# Night City Dispatch

Night City Dispatch is a **play-by-email** operations game set in the dark future. You don't roll dice — you **commit**.

Every round you submit orders. The city resolves them. There are no take-backs.

## Core Loop

1. Receive your **briefing** from the GM
2. Fill out the **Operation Grid** — assign crew to phases and columns
3. Pick **two actions** from your action slots
4. Submit your **order sheet** via email
5. The GM resolves all players simultaneously and sends the **dispatch**

## What You Need

- An email account
- A copy of this rulebook
- A GM who doesn't flinch
