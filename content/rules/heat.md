# Heat

Heat is persistent. It represents how visible and targeted you are across Night City.

## Heat Levels

| Level | Name | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| 0 | **Cold** | No penalties |
| 1 | **Warm** | All column scores −1 |
| 2 | **Hot** | All column scores −2 |
| 3 | **Burning** | All column scores −3, contacts go cold |
| 4+ | **Flatlined** | Active bounty, −4 to all |

## Heat Triggers

- Any **Strike** result of Poor or below: **+1**
- Any Flatlined or None phase outcome: **+1**
- Crew member captured or killed: **+2**

## Cooling Down

- Full dark operation (both slots on Ghost): **−1**
- Dedicated Heat suppression spend: **−1**
- Relationship maintenance action: **−1**

> Heat doesn't reset between rounds. It compounds. Manage it or get buried.
