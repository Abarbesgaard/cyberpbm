# Actions

Each round you get **2 action slots**. Actions are how you shape the operation beyond the grid.

## Available Actions

- **Deploy Asset** — Place a crew member or resource into a grid cell
- **Call Contact** — Reach out to a fixer, informant, or specialist
- **Run Net** — Execute a netrunning operation against a target
- **Go Loud** — Commit to a high-risk, high-reward strike
- **Lay Low** — Spend both slots to reduce Heat by 1
- **Bribe** — Spend eddies to shift a phase outcome
- **Recon** — Gather intel on a target before committing
- **Fortify** — Reinforce an Anchor position for the round

> Each action costs **one slot** unless noted otherwise. Choose wisely — you cannot undo a committed action.
