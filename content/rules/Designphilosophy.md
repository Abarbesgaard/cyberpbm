## I. Design Philosophy

The game is played via mails or in strictly goverend chat fora. The idea is to make a world where you dont have all the information you need to make the best decision there is, but if you align yourself with other players, hack into the right servers, or blow up the right building you just might get ahead of the competetion to rule nightcity!

This game revolves around *the operation grid*, you will be submitting this each round to your gamemaster, and then wait to see how the world responds to your actions. This Grid will be explained in detail in the dedicated section.

The Gm of this game will act as the city, making sure to breathe life into this world and make it believable. But dont worry there are ofcause LOTS of tables to roll on!
